My Process

A streamlined workflow to ensure quality and clarity from start to finish.

1. Consultation & Discovery

We start with a detailed discussion to understand your goals, target audience, and project requirements.

2. Strategy & Planning

I create a comprehensive plan, including a project roadmap, sitemap, and technology stack recommendations.

3. Design & Prototyping

Using tools like Figma, I design wireframes and high-fidelity mockups to visualize the user experience before development.

4. Development & Testing

The approved design is brought to life with clean, efficient code. I conduct rigorous testing to ensure everything is pixel-perfect.

5. Launch & Handover

After your final approval, I deploy the website to your server and provide you with all the necessary training and documentation.
